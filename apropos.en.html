Esthète \ɛs.tɛt\

Ancient Greek, Aisthêtês « Who perceives by the senses »

As far back as I can remember, I have always been fascinated by the craftsman who transforms the raw material and brings it to life with his know-how. I have always considered them as magicians, imagining them alone in their workshop with their precious tools making our needs, our desires, and turn our dreams in reality.

My first experience with leather definitely sealed my desire to work, to create with my hands and in turn to transmit the love and magic of the craftmanship. Today, I strive to integrate this magic into each of my creations, keeping in mind the very essence of craftsmanship: manual work, the right gesture, respect for the material.

This permanent need for creation takes me away from repetition, which is what drives me to offer my know-how to design a unique object for you and to be part of this tailor-made work by combining aesthetics and function.

Feel free to contact me for any project,

S.
